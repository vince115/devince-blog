## Introduction

The Time Traveller was expounding a recondite matter to us. His pale grey eyes shone and twinkled, and his usually pale face was flushed and animated. The fire burnt brightly, and the soft radiance of the incandescent lights in the lilies of silver caught the bubbles that flashed and passed in our glasses.

> “You must follow me carefully. I shall have to controvert one or two ideas that are almost universally accepted.”

He spoke, and we listened — not merely as to a story, but to a possibility.
